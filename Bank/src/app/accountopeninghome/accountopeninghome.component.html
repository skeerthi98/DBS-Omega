Online Opening of SB Account

With the benefits of convenience, time saving and security for our valued customers, our Bank has enabled online opening of SB account in simple easy steps.
  Pre-requisites for filling the online application :

  1. This facility is available only for resident Indians.
  2. The account can be opened for SELF mode of operation only.
  3. Soft copy of Passport size photographs (size should be less than 10 KB).
  4. KYC (Know Your Customer) documents :

    1. Scanned copy Photo ID
    2. Scanned copy of Address Proof (should be other than the photo ID)
      (The acceptable KYC documents are available through drop down menu in the online application)
    3. The size of each of the KYC document should be less than 500 KB.
  Process to Apply and Tracking :

The process to fill up the online application for opening a Savings Bank account in brief is as follows :
  1. Customer can choose any branch to open his new account and may provide nomination details also.
  2. The required documents viz. photograph, Photo ID and address proof as mentioned in online application are to be uploaded.
  3. A unique Tracking ID Number will be generated on submission of the application and the same is to be noted for future reference. 
  4. On successful submission of the application an email will be sent to the customer’s given email ID with unique tracking ID. Using this tracking ID, customer can track the application status.
  Note :
  1. Customers are advised to visit the chosen branch in person with original documents on T+2 working days and not later than 15 days from the date of submission of application.
  2. On scrutiny of the details of the customer, branch may approve / reject the application.
  3. On successful verification/approval of details, the new account will be opened in the Branch.
